Guess the BIRDLE in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid name for a bird. Hit the enter button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

The gray letters are not in the word in any spot. The gold letters like "H" and "Y" are in the word but in the wrong spot. The green letters like "M" and "N" are in the word and in the correct spot.

A new BIRDLE will be available each day!
